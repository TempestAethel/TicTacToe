Game Instructions

Welcome to Tic-Tac-Toe! Here’s how to play:

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Players take turns placing their mark (X or O) in an empty cell.
  • The first player to align three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins!
  • If all cells are filled and no player has three in a row, the game ends in a draw.

Select your game mode from the dropdown to start!
